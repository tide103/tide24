STEPS

  • Step 1: Check the dosing instructions on the packaging.
  • Step 2: Use the scoop to measure out the correct dose.
  • Step 3: If you’re using a top loader or a semi-automatic washing machine, dose detergent directly into the drum before adding clothes.
  • Step 4: If using a front loader, add the powder detergent to the drawer.
  • Step 5: Once you’ve added the detergent, place the clothes in the drum.
  • Step 6: Choose the cycle based on the recommendation on the fabric care label and press start.
